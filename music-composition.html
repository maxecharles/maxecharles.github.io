Where Is My Mine?

My friend Nelson and I decided to create an extremely high-effort, originally recorded Minecraft parody of "Where Is My Mind?" by the Pixies. If you live in the "likes Minecraft" and "likes the Pixies" Venn diagram intersection, then you will think this is the best thing ever, if you're just in one region of that Venn diagram, you will probably think it's kind of funny, and if you're in neither region you will probably think it's a bit weird.

Physics Lab Songs

Collaborating with lab coordinator Duncan Fisher at the University of Wollongong (and earlier with my high school physics teacher Mr. Woodley), I have written and recorded a series of songs to help students learn key physics concepts. These songs are used in labs preparation exercises to hopefully make it more engaging and memorable!
